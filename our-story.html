The Journey of a Single Grain

In the heartlands of India, nestled in the deltas of the Cauvery and the plains of the Deccan, lie stories waiting to be told. These are stories of soil, of seasons, and of seeds passed down through countless generations.

Isha Organics was born from a simple realization: the rice on most of our plates is a pale shadow of what it once was. Commercial agriculture has prioritized yield over nutrition, uniformity over diversity. In this process, we have lost thousands of indigenous rice varieties—each with a unique flavor, texture, and a wealth of health benefits.

We started by traveling to remote villages, speaking with elderly farmers who remembered growing these ancient grains. We found seeds for Karuppu Kavuni, the forbidden black rice of the Chettinad region, and Mappillai Samba, the red rice that was a rite of passage for Tamil grooms.

Our mission is twofold: to bring this incredible natural heritage to your table, and to create a sustainable livelihood for the farming communities who are the custodians of this legacy.

We don't just sell rice. We sell a piece of history, a commitment to health, and a promise to our planet.
